Flavia Di Pietro, PhD, is a National Health and Medical Research Council C.J. Martin Early Career Fellow in the School of Medical Sciences at The University of Sydney, Australia. Her PhD research investigated cortical representation in complex regional pain syndrome using brain imaging.
